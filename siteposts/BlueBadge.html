Bot 1: @PollBot

Link: [PollBot](https://t.me/PollBot)


Description: PollBot allows you to create and conduct polls within your Telegram groups or channels. Bot 2: @GIFsBot

Link: [GIFsBot](https://t.me/GIFsBot)


Description: Spice up your conversations with animated GIFs using the GIFsBot. This bot allows you to search and share GIFs directly within your Telegram chats. Whether you want to express an emotion, react to a message, or add some fun to your conversations, GIFsBot has got you covered with its extensive collection of animated GIFs.

Bot 3: @WeathermanBot

Link: [WeathermanBot](https://t.me/WeathermanBot)

With WeathermanBot, you'll always be prepared for any weather changes and plan your day accordingly.
